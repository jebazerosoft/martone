Biography of Martone

The inspiring journey of Martone, a visionary artist who rose from Lansing, Michigan, to become the Emperor of House Music.

The Emperor of House Music: Martone

Hailing from the capital city of Michigan, the Emperor of House Music, Martone, has made a significant impact in the music industry as a Pop/EDM recording artist. His album, #TEOM The Evolution Deluxe Edition, received critical acclaim shortly after its release, cementing his status in the music world. Martone’s notable chart-topping singles include Groove Tonight, Love You I Do, Office Politics, Chocolate | Spoken Word Mix, and Fuck Me Baby.

Early Beginnings and Passion for Entertainment

Martone’s journey in entertainment began at a young age. Growing up in Lansing, Michigan, he first discovered his passion after performing in a school play at just seven years old. The positive feedback from the audience played a crucial role in solidifying his determination to succeed in the entertainment industry. Known for his visionary approach, Martone is often described as an impassioned artist poised to become one of the most recognized names in the industry.

Chart Success and Musical Milestones

Martone is no stranger to success in the music world. On May 4, 2016, two singles produced by Marvin “DJ Extreme” Detroit Hairston, Love You I Do Auoo and Groove Tonight (Extreme’s Late Night Remix), entered the iTunes Fitness and Workout charts at #5 and #9, respectively. This achievement marked Martone’s first two consecutive top 10 hits on the iTunes charts.

On February 18, 2019, Martone collaborated with Clayton Morgan on the hit single Good Love, which debuted at #2 on the iTunes Electronic Dance Chart. This success earned Martone his third top-ten single on the iTunes charts, further establishing his influence in the electronic dance music scene.

Diverse Experience in Entertainment

Martone’s extensive experience in television, radio, and music has garnered him both recognition and respect. His multifaceted career has allowed him to build a diverse portfolio, showcasing his talents across different media platforms. This broad range of experience contributes to his unique style and ability to connect with a wide audience.

Additional Achievements

In addition to his chart successes, Martone has continued to innovate and expand his musical repertoire. He has performed at numerous events and collaborated with various artists, further solidifying his presence in the music industry. His dedication to his craft and his ability to adapt to changing musical trends have made him a standout artist in the Pop/EDM genre.

Martone’s journey from a young performer in Lansing, Michigan, to an acclaimed recording artist serves as an inspiration to aspiring musicians and entertainers. His passion, vision, and relentless pursuit of excellence continue to drive his success, making him a true Emperor of House Music.

Martone was nominated for the Outstanding Electronic/Dance Recording category at the 2020 Detroit Music Awards. This recognition highlights his contributions to the electronic music scene in Detroit, which has a rich history of fostering talent in genres like house and techno. However, the award that year went to “Pure Sounds of Michigan” by Various Artists.

Detroit Music Awards – 2020 Nominee for Outstanding Electronic Recording

In 2020, Martone’s album “Erogenous Zone” was honored with a nomination for Outstanding Electronic Recording at the prestigious Detroit Music Awards. This nomination places Martone in a distinguished group of artists recognized for their innovation and creativity within the electronic music genre.

The Detroit Music Awards celebrate the exceptional musical talents originating from Detroit and its surrounding regions, and being considered for this award showcases Martone’s dedication to creating boundary-pushing and soulful electronic music.

“It was such a privilege to be nominated for Outstanding Electronic Recording at the 2020 Detroit Music Awards. Being recognized alongside such talented artists is a tremendous honor. Unfortunately, due to the global pandemic, we were not able to attend the awards ceremony, but the nomination itself is a testament to the hard work and passion that went into creating Erogenous Zone. Detroit’s music community has always been a source of inspiration for me, and this recognition only strengthens my connection to the city’s vibrant and historic music scene.”

- Martone

Martone received the Silver Medal for Outstanding Achievement in Electronic Dance Music at the Global Music Awards in November 2019. This recognition highlights his exceptional creativity and artistry in the electronic dance music genre, placing him among an elite group of internationally recognized independent artists.

Global Music Awards – Silver Medal for Outstanding Achievement in Electronic Dance Music (November 2019)

In November 2019, Martone earned a prestigious Silver Medal for Outstanding Achievement from the Global Music Awards in the category of Electronic Dance Music. The Global Music Awards is known for celebrating the artistic achievement, creativity, and talent of independent artists across various genres of music.

This recognition places Martone among a select group of artists who have demonstrated excellence in their musical craft and have made significant contributions to the global music landscape.

“I am deeply honored to receive this Silver Medal from the Global Music Awards. This recognition is not just a personal milestone but a testament to the hard work, dedication, and passion that went into creating music that speaks to the soul. Electronic Dance Music has always been a part of my heart, and to be recognized on this level is incredibly rewarding. I want to extend my gratitude to everyone who believed in me and supported this journey.”

- Martone

Martone was honored as a Finalist for Best Pop Song at the World Songwriting Awards in Fall 2020 for his track “Love Out Loud.” This recognition highlights his unique ability to blend heartfelt storytelling with pop sensibility, creating songs that connect deeply with audiences worldwide.

World Songwriting Awards – Finalist for Best Pop Song (Fall 2020)

Martone was named a Finalist for Best Pop Song at the World Songwriting Awards in Fall 2020 for his track “Love Out Loud.” The World Songwriting Awards is a respected global platform that recognizes outstanding independent songwriters whose music demonstrates originality, creativity, and impact.

This achievement underscores Martone’s exceptional songwriting skills and his ability to craft music that combines emotional depth with pop-driven melodies.

“Being a finalist for Best Pop Song with ‘Love Out Loud’ at the World Songwriting Awards is such an incredible honor. Songwriting has always been a deeply personal experience for me, and to have my work recognized among such talented artists is truly humbling. ‘Love Out Loud’ is a song about expression, self-acceptance, and the power of love, and I hope it continues to inspire others as much as it has inspired me.”

- Martone

Deep & Raw: The Erotica of Martone

Martone has always been a multi-faceted artist, pushing boundaries in music, fashion, and entertainment. As The Emperor of House Music, he has captivated audiences worldwide with his electrifying beats and dynamic performances. Now, he expands his artistry into the literary world with the release of his debut book, Deep & Raw: The Erotica of Martone.

“I’ve always believed that art should push boundaries, provoke thought, and stir emotions. Deep & Raw is a deeply personal project that explores passion, sensuality, and the power of desire through storytelling. As an artist, I’ve used music to express my truth, and now, through literature, I’m bringing a new dimension of my creativity to the world. This book is about embracing who you are, owning your fantasies, and celebrating unapologetic self-expression. I can’t wait for readers to experience this journey with me.”

- Martone

2025: Chocolate Charts in the UK

On March 13, 2025, Chocolate (Drumapella Mix) became a Top 20 hit on the UK Electronic Dance Charts, a full 10 years after its initial release. This stripped-down version of Chocolate (Spoken Word Mix) continues to showcase Martone’s enduring influence in the dance music scene.

Following the success of his single charting at #14 on the UK iTunes Dance Chart, Martone continues to break barriers and redefine what it means to be an artist in full command of his truth. With bold storytelling, unapologetic sensuality, and a sound that’s as fierce as it is unforgettable, Martone stands tall as The Emperor of House Music.

Amazon Best Seller – Deep & Raw

Martone’s latest literary work, Deep & Raw: The Erotica of Martone, has captivated audiences across the globe, climbing to #3 on Amazon’s Best Sellers in African Poetry.

The collection is a provocative and deeply personal dive into desire, identity, and freedom — solidifying Martone’s place as a multidimensional creative force.

Whether on stage, in the studio, or on the page, Martone blends artistry with authenticity, turning every experience into a statement. This moment is just the beginning.

Martone continues to innovate and redefine what it means to be an artist, ensuring that his voice—whether through music or literature—remains a powerful force of expression and liberation.
