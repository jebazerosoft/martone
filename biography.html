Biography

The Emperor of House Music

About Martone

Martone, known as "The Emperor of House Music," is a multifaceted artist who has made significant contributions to the music industry as well as literature. With a career spanning music production, authorship, television hosting, and radio personality work, Martone represents the epitome of creative versatility.

As an accomplished author, Martone has penned several bestselling works, including "Deep & Raw: The Erotica of Martone," which reached #3 on Amazon's African Poetry bestsellers list. His literary prowess extends to poetry collections and prose that explore themes of identity, desire, and artistic expression.

In the music world, Martone has established himself as a formidable force in house music, with tracks like "Love You I Do" reaching #5 on iTunes Fitness charts and "Chocolate (Drumapella Mix)" climbing the UK Electronic Dance Charts even a decade after its release.

Beyond music and literature, Martone's experience as a former TV show host and radio personality has given him a unique platform to connect with audiences across multiple mediums, cementing his status as a true multimedia entertainer.
