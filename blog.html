2025 Usage Guidelines: Martone's Image, Likeness, and Brand Identity

2025 Usage Guidelines Official Trademark of Martone™ NOTICE FROM LEGAL REPRESENTATIVES OF MARTONE INC This statement establishes the official 2025 guidelines regarding the use, licensing and protections associated with the intellectual property, image, and brand identity of Martone — the artist, author, and creative visionary.

As we enter 2025, it's important to establish clear guidelines for the use of my name, image, likeness, and brand identity. This comprehensive statement outlines the official policies regarding intellectual property protection and authorized usage rights.

Trademark Protection

The name "Martone" and associated branding elements are protected trademarks. Any commercial use requires prior written authorization from our legal representatives.

Image and Likeness Rights

All photographs, videos, and other media featuring Martone are subject to strict usage rights. Media outlets and content creators must obtain proper licensing for any commercial or promotional use.

Authorized Usage

Fans and supporters are welcome to share officially released content for non-commercial purposes, with proper attribution. Educational and journalistic use is permitted under fair use guidelines.

For licensing inquiries or questions about usage rights, please contact our legal team at legal@martoneonline.com
