Music Availability

Update on My Music Availability and Exciting News

Recently, my distribution contract with Label-Worx ended, which led to the removal of my music from platforms like Apple Music, Spotify, and Deezer. Given this situation, I took the opportunity to not only redistribute my music but also refresh the artwork for my singles and albums. I’m excited to share that The Evolution of Martone: Deluxe Edition, Rippin’ the Runway, Fever, Fuck Me Baby, and Love Out Loud are now available again on various platforms.

In even more exciting news, I’m currently working on new music that will be released next year. Since my last project, I’ve written over 100 songs, and I’ll be heading into the studio in December to start recording. I’m also in the process of assembling an amazing team to help bring this new music to life, and I can’t wait to share the magic we create with you.

Over the next few months, you’ll notice some changes to my website and social media presence. It’s been five years since my last album, and I have a lot to say. Be sure to follow and subscribe to all of my social media accounts so you stay up to date with everything going on. Your support means the world to me, and I’m looking forward to this next chapter!
