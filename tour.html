Experience The Emperor Live

Join Martone on his electrifying tour as The Emperor of House Music brings his signature sound to stages worldwide. Each performance is a unique journey through the evolution of house music, featuring both classic hits and unreleased tracks.

From intimate club venues to massive festival stages, Martone's live performances are renowned for their energy, creativity, and connection with the audience. Experience the power of house music as it was meant to be heard - live and unfiltered.

What to Expect

  • Chart-topping hits including "Love You I Do" and "Chocolate"
  • Exclusive live versions and extended mixes
  • Interactive performance elements
  • Meet and greet opportunities
  • Limited edition tour merchandise
