🎤 Martone Live from Platinum Keyz Studios! 🎤
Exclusive Online Streaming Event

Martone, The Emperor of House Music, is back! After years away from the stage, Martone will be performing live on Friday, January 17, 2025, at 12:15 p.m. from Platinum Keyz Studios in a special closed session exclusively for online streaming.

🎶 What to Expect:

  • Two live performances of Martone's favorites
  • An intimate Q&A session where Martone answers your questions

Martone shared: "I definitely feel like I owe my supporters something in terms of a live performance. It has been since August 2017, according to BandsInTown, that I have done a performance. I am actively seeking performance opportunities as this is something that I love to do, but it certainly has to be right on so many levels."

📅 Date: Friday, January 17, 2025
🕛 Time: 12:15 p.m.
